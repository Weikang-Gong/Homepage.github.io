Brief Biography

Mr. Weikang Gong received his B.S. degree in Electronic and Information Engineering from Qingdao University of Science and Technology. From 2015, he acted as a master student of Faculty of Environmental and Life Sciences in Beijing University of Technology and a member of Bioinformatics Lab, led by Prof. Dr. Chunhua Li. He entered in the Ph.D study phase in 2017. He is currently a Ph.D student. He is interested in the following topics:

  • Protein/RNA/Protein-RNA Complex Structure-Dynamics Relationship

  • Protein-Protein Interaction Network and Complex Structure Prediction

  • Protein-RNA Recognition and Interaction
